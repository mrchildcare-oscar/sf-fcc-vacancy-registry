# SF FCC Vacancy Registry: Marketing & Sustainability Plan

## Executive Summary

This plan outlines a strategy to grow the SF Family Child Care Vacancy Registry with a $500/month marketing budget while building toward long-term sustainability. The platform serves as both a community resource and a proof-of-concept that can be licensed to other FCC associations nationwide.

---

## Part 1: Strategic Positioning

### Our Competitive Advantage Over Winnie

| Factor | Winnie | SF FCC Registry |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Focus | All childcare types | FCC specialists |
| Trust | Generic platform | Association-backed |
| Cost to providers | Paid features | Free |
| Local knowledge | Algorithm-based | Community-curated |
| Provider support | None | Association membership |
| Language support | English-focused | EN/ES/中文 |

**Key Message:** "The only childcare registry built BY Family Child Care providers, FOR families."

### Target Audiences

**Primary:**
1. **Parents** - Seeking infant/toddler care in SF (hardest to find)
2. **FCC Providers** - Looking to fill vacancies quickly

**Secondary:**
3. **Other FCC Associations** - Potential platform licensees
4. **Funders/Grantmakers** - Childcare access initiatives

## Part 2: Marketing Strategy ($500/month)

### Budget Allocation

| Channel | Monthly Budget | Purpose |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| Google Ads | $200 | High-intent parent searches |
| Meta Ads (FB/IG) | $150 | Awareness + provider recruitment |
| Print/Offline | $100 | Flyers, QR codes, event materials |
| Reserve/Testing | $50 | A/B testing, seasonal pushes |

### Online Strategy

#### Google Ads ($200/month)

**Why Google:** Parents actively searching for childcare have high intent. They're ready to act.

**Target Keywords (Long-tail, lower competition):**
- "family child care san francisco"
- "infant care sf"
- "home daycare san francisco"
- "licensed childcare near me sf"
- "spanish speaking daycare san francisco"
- "chinese speaking childcare sf"

**Expected Performance:**
- Average CPC for childcare: $2-4 (long-tail keywords)
- $200 budget = ~50-100 clicks/month
- Estimated conversion rate: 10-20%
- Result: 5-20 new parent inquiries/month

**Ad Copy Examples:**
```
Find Licensed Family Child Care in SF
✓ Verified Providers ✓ Real-Time Openings
✓ Free for Families | Search Now
```

```
Infant Care Openings in San Francisco
Home-based childcare with real availability
Backed by SF Family Child Care Association
```

#### Meta Ads - Facebook/Instagram ($150/month)

**Why Meta:** Reach parents before they're actively searching. Build awareness.

**Audience Targeting:**
- Location: San Francisco + 10 mile radius
- Age: 25-45
- Interests: Parenting, pregnancy, childcare, working parents
- Behaviors: Parents with children 0-5

**Campaign Types:**

1. **Parent Awareness Campaign** ($100/month)
   - Carousel ad showing diverse providers
   - Video testimonial from a parent
   - "Looking for childcare? Skip the waitlists."

2. **Provider Recruitment Campaign** ($50/month)
   - Target: FCC providers not yet on platform
   - "List your openings for free"
   - Reach providers through parenting groups

**Expected Performance:**
- CPM (cost per 1000 views): $8-15
- $150 = 10,000-18,000 impressions
- CTR: 1-2% = 100-360 clicks
- Builds brand recognition over time

#### Free/Low-Cost Online Tactics

1. **Google Business Profile**
   - Create listing for "SF FCC Vacancy Registry"
   - Appears in local searches
   - Free

2. **SEO Content**
   - Blog: "How to Find Family Child Care in SF"
   - Blog: "FCC vs Daycare Centers: What Parents Should Know"
   - Builds organic traffic over time

3. **Social Media (Organic)**
   - Share provider spotlights
   - Post childcare tips
   - Celebrate new providers joining

4. **Nextdoor**
   - Post in SF neighborhoods
   - Free, hyper-local reach

5. **Parent Facebook Groups**
   - SF Parents groups
   - Neighborhood-specific groups
   - Must be helpful, not spammy

### Offline Strategy ($100/month)

#### Tabling Events

**High-Value Events:**
- Farmer's markets (weekend foot traffic)
- Library story times
- Community resource fairs
- School enrollment events
- WIC offices (partnership)

**Materials Needed:**
- Tablecloth with branding
- Flyers (bilingual EN/ES/中文)
- QR code standee → direct to vacancy page
- Tablet for live demos
- Small giveaway (stickers, magnets with QR code)

**Print Budget Breakdown:**
- 500 flyers/month: $50
- QR code standee (one-time): $30
- Stickers/magnets: $20/month

#### Strategic Flyer Placement

**High-traffic locations:**
- Pediatrician offices (ask to leave flyers)
- Family resource centers
- Libraries
- Laundromats in family neighborhoods
- Churches with family programs
- Preschools (for school-age care)

#### QR Code Strategy

Create trackable QR codes for each channel:
- `beta.familychildcaresf.com/?ref=flyer-mission`
- `beta.familychildcaresf.com/?ref=farmers-market`
- `beta.familychildcaresf.com/?ref=pediatrician`

This lets you measure which offline efforts work best.
