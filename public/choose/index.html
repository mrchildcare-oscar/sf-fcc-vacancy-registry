What Matters Most When Choosing Child Care?

Based on a 2026 survey of 312 San Francisco families

Home › Trust Wheel

Every family weighs different factors when choosing child care. In 2026, the Family Child Care Association of San Francisco surveyed 312 families across the city — English, Spanish, and Chinese-speaking — to learn what matters most. We found 8 recurring trust factors that shape how parents evaluate and choose care. We call this the Trust Wheel.

Explore each factor below to see what the data says, how families in different communities prioritize it, and how to evaluate providers on the things that matter most to you.
